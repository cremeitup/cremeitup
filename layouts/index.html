The Studio

Crafted by
Anushka Madaan

With over 5 years of pastry experience, Chef Anushka Madaan creates desserts that balance artistry with flavour. Trained in French pastry techniques, she brings a unique blend of European finesse and Indian warmth to every creation.

Every item at Crème It Up is baked fresh at our Sector 46C studio in Chandigarh using premium ingredients: French butter, Belgian chocolate, and organic flour. No preservatives, no shortcuts just honest, handcrafted baking.

Beyond the oven, Anushka teaches the craft through intimate workshops that have trained hundreds of aspiring bakers. Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned home baker, there's a class designed for you.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver cakes in Chandigarh, Mohali, and Panchkula?

Yes, we deliver custom cakes and dessert boxes across Chandigarh, Mohali, Panchkula, and Zirakpur. For areas outside the tricity, please contact us on WhatsApp and we'll do our best to accommodate your order.

What flavours and designs are available for custom cakes?

We offer a wide range of flavours including an OG chocolate cake, fresh berry cake, chocolate and hazelnut, and many more. For designs, the sky is the limit -from elegant minimalist cakes to elaborate themed creations. Share your inspiration and we'll design something perfect for your occasion.

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

We recommend placing your order at least 3-5 days in advance for custom cakes. For elaborate wedding cakes or large orders, 1-2 weeks notice is ideal. Last-minute orders may be possible depending on availability -just reach out and we'll try our best.

What baking classes do you offer?

We offer short and long duration courses in person at our Sector 46C Chandigarh studio. Check our Classes page for details.

Can beginners join your baking workshops?

Yes, our workshops are designed for all skill levels. Most of our students are beginners, and Chef Anushka teaches every technique step by step. You don't need any prior baking experience -just a willingness to learn. We keep class sizes small so everyone gets personal attention and guidance.

What ingredients do you use?

We use only premium, high-quality ingredients -imported French butter, Belgian couverture chocolate, Madagascar vanilla, and organic flour. We never use artificial flavours, colours, or preservatives. Every item is baked fresh on the day of delivery or class.

How can I place an order or book a workshop?

The easiest way is to message us on WhatsApp or Instagram. You can also email us at Cremeitup@gmail.com or visit our Contact page. We typically respond within a few hours.
