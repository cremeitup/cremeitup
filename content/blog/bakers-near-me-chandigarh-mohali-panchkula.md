## The Tricity Bakery Landscape

The Chandigarh-Mohali-Panchkula tricity has seen a boom in bakeries over the last few years. You can broadly categorize them:

### Chain Bakeries
Available everywhere, consistent quality, affordable pricing. Good for everyday bread and basic cakes.

### Boutique / Home Bakeries
Chef-driven, small-batch, premium ingredients. Where you go for custom cakes, artisan breads, and specialty pastries. These are the hidden gems that don't always show up on Google Maps.

### Café-Bakeries
Coffee shops with a baking component. Good for pastries with your latte, but often not their core focus.

## How to Find Good Bakers Near You

1. **Search Instagram first** — Hashtags like #chandigarhbakery, #mohalifoodies, #panchkulacakes show real local bakers
2. **Check Google Reviews** — But read the actual reviews, not just the star rating
3. **Ask in local groups** — Chandigarh foodie groups on Facebook are goldmines for recommendations
4. **Try small first** — Order a box of cookies or a small cake before committing to a wedding order
