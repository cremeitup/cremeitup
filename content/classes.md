## Our Workshops

Learn the art of baking from **Chef Anushka Madaan** -hands-on workshops designed for all skill levels. Whether you're just starting out or looking to refine advanced techniques, our classes bring real pastry skills to your kitchen.

We offer long and short duration courses.

### What You'll Learn

Our **4 Weeks Baking Workshop** covers everything from basic techniques to advanced pastry arts:

- **Cookies** -Gooey cookies, short crust pastries, centre filled cookies and more
- **Travel Cakes** -Chocolate and vanilla marbled tea cake, muffins, brownies and more
- **Cake Decorating** -Buttercream/whipped cream, different types of fillings, modern minimalist designs, cheesecakes and more
- **Bread Making** -Soft rolls, Korean garlic buns, focaccia and more
- **Chocolate Work** -Tempering, chocolate garnishes, ganaches
- **Dessert Styling** -Garnishing, presentation, buffet presentation techniques

### Workshop Formats

- **In-Person** -Intimate studio sessions at our Sector 46C, Chandigarh location
- **Private Sessions** -One-on-one or small group classes tailored to your goals

### Who Should Join?

Whether you're a complete beginner looking for your first baking class, or an experienced baker wanting to refine your skills -our workshops are designed for all levels. We keep class sizes small for personal attention.
